Customer mission

We are a small family-run local business, operating in Kirwan since 2012, hoping to
improve our business by getting on the Internet.

Goal

Get customers used to the new location of the bakery.
Get new customers to the store and hooked to the bakery.

Success evaluation

Measure the website users and the according increase in revenue or visitors to
the actual bakery.
Increase in sales of the pastry displayed on the website to additional pastry.

Target audience

We have customers of all ages, however, we would like to attract young families
and young people, possibly around the age of 17 – 35, to our bakery.

bright, happy colours is to attract kids and thus their parents to the bakery.
yellow and pink in pastel is a magnet for young people.
For teenagers a simplistic modern look is all it needs to get them hooked.
